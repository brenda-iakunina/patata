MEET OUR THERAPIST

Liana, a Certified Massage Therapist, has many years of experience in massage beauty centers, in spa and conducts massage sessions at home, in a hotel or at your office, by having professional equipment and a professional massage bed. She has attended many seminars on "Release and Relax Motion Massage" and seminars on "Living as Elderly" and "Physical Therapy in the Third Age".
