SWEDISH MASSAGE

Swedish massage is the most commonly used massage technique that uses constant or light pressure on the muscles. Swedish massage is mainly used to increase blood flow, relaxes the muscles of the body and improves its flexibility. This type of massage also stimulates the nervous system.

RELAXING MASSAGE

Relaxing massage is a form of massage that promotes general relaxation, relieves muscle tension, improves circulation and range of motion. DEEP TISSUE

Deep tissue massage is aimed at deeper muscle structures. It is usually used in minor muscle damage and in chronic problems. Athlete injuries, straight body posture, and muscle strain are all good examples of what a deep tissue massage could best target. The therapist will use the fingers, the thumbs, the fist, the elbows and the arms, focusing on releasing chronic muscle tension.
